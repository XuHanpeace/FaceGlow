Guideline 2.1 - Face Data Collection
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Issue: Need detailed information about face data usage.

Responses:

Question 1: What face data does the app collect?

- Facial images (user-uploaded selfies)
- Facial feature points (landmarks, contours for AI processing)
- Generated results (AI-beautified photos)

Question 2: Uses of face data:

- AI face-swapping: Apply facial features to artistic templates
- Beautification: Enhance photo quality with AI filters
- Template matching: Match features with style templates
- Algorithm optimization: De-identified data only (cannot identify individuals)

Question 3: Third-party sharing and storage:

Tencent Cloud COS:
- Purpose: Store uploaded photos and generated works
- Location: Mainland China servers
- Transfer: HTTPS encrypted
- Security: Enterprise-level protection

AI Processing Service:
- Purpose: Execute AI algorithms
- Usage: Current session only, not retained

We NEVER:
- Sell face data to third parties
- Publicly display photos without consent
- Use face data for advertising
- Transfer data to other companies

Question 4: Data retention periods:

- Uploaded selfies: Until user deletion
- Temporary processing photos: Deleted immediately after processing
- Facial feature data: Deleted within 24 hours
- Generated works: Until user deletion; 30 days after account cancellation
- De-identified data: Up to 2 years (completely anonymized)

Question 5: Privacy Policy location:

URL: https://xuhanpeace.github.io/facegolow-support/privacy-policy-en.html

Specific sections:
- Section 1.1: "Facial Data Collection"
- Section 2: "Purpose of Using Facial Data"
